Dysarthria Diagnosis App

Using AI to assist with speech disorder diagnosis and treatment

In this project, our goal is to use machine learning to detect dysarthria from speech across three different languages, to classify its severity using spectrogram data, detect the emotion of a signal, and see if we can convert a dysarthric speech signal to nondysarthric speech using generative models. This could aid clinicians in both diagnosis and ongoing assessment.
